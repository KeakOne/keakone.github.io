About

I'm a Web developer & Designer located in Wellington, New Zealand. I specialise in designing & creating websites for individuals and small businesses.

Creating a website to get your message across can be a big challenge. I'm here to make the process as easy for you as possible- from deciding on initial goals and what you want your website to say about you; to coding and getting your website online for the world to see!

whether you have your website design in mind already, or dont know where to start but recognise its needed, I can work with you face-to-face to help you understand how the modern web works and the most popular and low-cost strategies for getting your message across.
