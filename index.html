About

I'm a web designer & developer in Wellington, New Zealand. I have two years of (formal) web experience, and am still loving learning new technologys everyday. I'm a big believer that constantly coding and having fun experimenting with what personally grabs you the most. I have a great knowledge of HTML5, CSS/SASS, Javascript and a few of its framework-friends(React.js, Node.js & Angular.js), that I put to use on creating fun websites.


My goal is too be an all around digital designer- someone who can carry a project from first wireframes, to polished online experience.

If you'd like to learn a lil' bit more have a look at my resume , or better yet send me an email.
