Whats the deal with 'scope' in Javascript?

Scope is used when you talk about something being available to a portion of the program, or the entire program. There are two scopes, global and local. Global means that whatever is set in that scope, normally outside of a function, is available to all code written inthat file. Local scope is used to restrict information, or processes to a certain function. You do this by placing the variable or function inside or outside of the parenthesis of the program.
